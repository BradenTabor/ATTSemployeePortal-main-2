# Groundsman Certification — Study Guide

## Overview

This guide covers key topics for the Groundsman certification test. Use it alongside hands-on training.

## JSA & Documentation

- Fill out JSA **prior to start of work**, for separate tickets, and before new circuits
- **NOT** required for each break
- All personnel on site must sign JSA (workers, General Foreman, O.C.)
- DVIR form filled out **daily**
- Report equipment problems to General Foreman **immediately**

## Safety Distances

| Distance | Application |
|----------|-------------|
| 2 spans | Stay away from downed power lines |
| 10 feet | MAD for non-qualified personnel / Distance from chainsaw operator |
| 300 feet | Buffer from tree trimmer and mulching equipment |
| 360 degrees | Circle of awareness while driving |
| 3 points | Contact while mounting/dismounting |

## Chipper Operations

### Feeding
- Feed from the **curb side** only
- Use **push stick** to push debris into hopper
- Never use hands or body

### Startup Sequence
1. Reverse feed wheels
2. Ensure clutch is disengaged
3. Start and warm up
4. Engage clutch, idle up, forward feed wheels

### Shutdown Sequence
1. Reverse feed wheels
2. Idle down engine
3. Disengage clutch
4. Shut off engine, remove keys

### Required PPE
- Hard hat
- Safety glasses
- Ear protection
- Gloves
- Safety vest

## Chainsaw Safety

- Never operate above your **waist**
- Stay **10 feet** from anyone operating a chainsaw
- Chaps **NOT** required in bucket (but required on ground)
- Never position in kickback zone

### Chainsaw Safety Features
- Chain catch
- Muffler
- Chain brake

### Chainsaw PPE
- Hard hat
- Ear plugs
- Gloves
- Safety glasses
- Chainsaw chaps (ground work)

## Tree Felling

- Notch should be **1/3** diameter of tree
- Verbal warning before: tree falls, cutting starts, chainsaw starts
- Never in kickback zone

## Traffic Control

### Signs Required Per Truck
- Utility Work Ahead
- Lane Closed Ahead
- Flagger Ahead

### Cone Spacing
- **10 feet** baseline
- Add **1 foot per 5 MPH** over 45 mph

### Zone Requirements
All four zones must be marked on highways:
1. Termination Zone
2. Work Zone
3. Buffer Zone
4. Taper Zone

### Rules
- Must put signs on dirt/gravel roads too
- Cannot leave signs while helping another crew
- Cone off driveways accessing highways
- Wheel chocks required on inclines

## Herbicide Safety

- **Never** spray near ponds, lakes, rivers, or streams
- **Must** wash skin if contacted
- Required PPE: Safety glasses, vest, gloves, mask, hard hat, long sleeves

## Aerial Rescue

### Purpose
Practice aerial rescue to be prepared in stressful situations to complete rescue safely and quickly.

### CHECK, CALL, CARE
1. **Check** — Area for hazards
2. **Call** — 911 or General Foreman
3. **Care** — For injured person

### If Person in MAD
Call: General Foreman, 911, O.C. (all of them)

## Working with Equipment Crews

As groundman with tree trimmer/mulching crew:
- Cut 10-foot buffer around poles and guy wires
- Mark obstacles with high-visibility flagging tape
- Maintain 300-foot buffer from coworkers and pedestrians

## Vehicle Safety

- Seat belts required anytime vehicle moves
- 360-degree awareness while driving
- Avoid backing when possible
- When unsure backing: **get out and look**

## Proper Lifting

- Bend at **knees**, not back
- Keep large logs/brush from high traffic areas (prevents trips, emergency access)

## Practice

Take the certification test from **Resources → Certifications** when you're ready.
