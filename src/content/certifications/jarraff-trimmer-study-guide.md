# Jarraff Trimmer Certification — Study Guide

## Overview

This guide covers key topics for the Jarraff tree trimmer certification test. Use it alongside hands-on training.

## Safety Distances

| Distance | Application |
|----------|-------------|
| 2.5 feet | Minimum approach distance with saw blade |
| 6 feet | From ledges, drop offs, and marked hazards |
| 10 feet | Buffer zone around poles and guy wires |
| 2 spans | Stay away from downed power lines |
| 300 feet | Personnel buffer zone during operation |
| 25 degrees | Maximum sideling ground operation |

## Safety Features

Know these Jarraff trimmer safety features:

1. **Door** — Cab protection
2. **Grab handles** — Safe mounting/dismounting
3. **Backup beeper** — Alert when reversing
4. **Backup camera** — Safe reversing visibility
5. **Saw guard** — Blade protection

## Degree Indicators

Located in two places:
- **Inside the cab**
- **On the boom cradle**

## Critical Safety Rules

- **Never** operate with cab door open
- **Never** trim trees directly above the trimmer
- **Never** trim without clear view of drop zone
- **No passengers** allowed in or on trimmer
- **Stop immediately** if anyone enters 300ft buffer zone
- **Wait for blade to completely stop** before exiting cab

## Blade & Maintenance

- Check blade teeth **every day**
- Sharp blade = more control over limbs
- Grease moving parts **every 8 hours**

## Responsibilities

### Hazard Identification
**Both** operator and groundman are responsible

### JSA
**Both** groundman and operator fill out daily JSA

### Line of Sight
Groundman must maintain visual contact with operator at all times

## Downed Power Lines

If you find a downed power line:
1. Back away **two spans**
2. Notify the machine operator
3. Notify anyone else in the area
4. Call General Foreman and O.C.

## Practical Evaluation Areas

### Machine Startup
- Equipment inspection
- JSA completion
- Proper PPE
- Fueling
- Warm-up time
- Function test

### Trimming Observation
- Machine positioning
- Safety feature usage
- Safe trimming practices
- Communication (operator/groundman)
- 300ft distance maintained
- Line of sight maintained
- 10ft buffer from poles/guy wires

### Shutdown
- Park in suitable location
- Idle down with blade disengaged
- **Wait for blade to stop** before exiting
- Boom cradled properly
- Debris/fire hazards removed
- Shutdown maintenance completed
