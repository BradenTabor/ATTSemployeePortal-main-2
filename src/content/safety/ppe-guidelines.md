# Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Guidelines

## Required PPE by Task

### General Line Work

- **Hard hat** — Class E rated
- **Safety glasses** — ANSI Z87.1 rated
- **High-visibility vest** — Class 2 or higher
- **Work boots** — Steel toe, EH rated
- **Gloves** — Appropriate for task (leather, rubber, cut-resistant)

### Bucket / Aerial Work

All general PPE plus:

- **Full-body harness** — Inspected daily
- **Lanyard** — 6 ft max with shock absorber
- **Dielectric overshoes** — When working near energized lines

### Tree Felling / Chainsaw

All general PPE plus:

- **Chainsaw chaps** — Cut-resistant
- **Face shield** or mesh visor
- **Hearing protection** — When operating loud equipment

## PPE Inspection Checklist

Before each use, inspect for:

- [ ] Cracks, cuts, or damage
- [ ] Frayed straps or stitching
- [ ] Proper fit and adjustment
- [ ] Expiration dates (hard hats, harnesses)
- [ ] Clean and functional condition

## Care and Storage

1. **Clean** PPE after use — remove dirt, sweat, chemicals
2. **Store** in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
3. **Replace** damaged or expired equipment immediately
4. **Never modify** PPE — use as designed

## Reporting Defective PPE

If PPE is damaged or defective:

1. Remove it from service immediately
2. Tag it as "Do Not Use"
3. Report to supervisor
4. Request replacement

## Remember

**PPE is your last line of defense.** Use it properly every time.
